Shiro

Ethiopian common dish, shiro wet

shiro wet is a sweet dish which
most ethiopian eat in a daily base.

It tests amazing when you eat it
when it is hot!

Ingredients

  • chickpea/shiro flour
  • onions
  • oil
  • water
  • salt

Steps

  1. bring stockpot to a heat
  2. add chooped onion, mix & wait
  3. pour oil and mix
  4. pour enough water
  5. when boiled, add shiro flour
  6. mix with spoon
  7. after some times bring it out
  8. Ofcourse, enjoy the meal!
